Our Solution for a Greener Tomorrow

Our application is a personalized recommendation system designed to enhance soil health, optimize fertilizer use, and improve crop yield.



Features

Order management

Handle all your sales and purchases activities, manage invoices and bills, and track payments. InvoSmart also helps you monitor packages and shipments to keep your deliveries on time.

Barcode scanning

InvoSmart is compatible with different types of barcode scanners which makes it easy to set up a barcode system and add SKI-JS, serial and batch numbers, and items to sales and purchase orders.

Inventory tracking

You can keep track of spare parts, saleable items, and even expiry dates with the serial and batch tracking feature of Zoho Inventory.

Reports

Know your inventory aging, vendor payments, sales details and inventory valuation from a range of reports which can be generated, downloaded and shared easily.
